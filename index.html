Executive Summary

Four departments audited, consistent pattern: capable people doing manual work while AI tools sit unused.

SEM: 30-40 team members manually review 40,000 keywords weekly. Creative/Paid Social: Both cite creative briefs as their biggest time drain (20 min to 2+ hours each), causing cross-department bottlenecks. Client Experience: Every call may contain sentiment triggers and upsell signals, but those are not tracked systematically.

Bottom line: LSG already pays for Gemini 2.5 Pro, an excellent frontier model. Teams just don't know what modern AI can actually do. With training and low-hanging AI-powered automation, LSG can become a truly AI-forward agency that's better future-proofed with higher margins.
